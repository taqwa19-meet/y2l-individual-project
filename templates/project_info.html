About us

Often we hear about violence against women, men and women as one are affected by these accidents.
This website is a safe place where women and men can share experiences about times where they were victims for violence, seek help, help each other and say whatever is on their mind.
vision:by building this website i aspire to give every person the safe space to confess any experience they had, help each other, create a society where people feel safe to fight violence against women and decrease it as much as possible.
